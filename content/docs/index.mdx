EarnIT is yield infrastructure for fintechs. Your users deposit a stablecoin; EarnIT routes it into Circle's **USYC** (a tokenized US-treasury fund) through ERC-4626 vaults on **Arc**, and accrues yield back into their balance. You integrate once and earn a share of the yield your users generate.

You never build custody, oracles, smart contracts, or a compliance function. EarnIT owns the protocol layer — you ship the experience.

<Callout type="info">
  EarnIT is currently live on **Arc testnet**. Do not move mainnet funds through it until the core contracts have completed a third-party audit.
</Callout>

## How it fits together

- **SDK & API** — `@earnit/sdk` and a REST API handle deposits, withdrawals, positions, and webhooks.
- **Embeddable widget** — a white-label deposit/withdraw card you drop into your app.
- **Protocol** — non-custodial ERC-4626 vaults that settle into USYC on Arc.
