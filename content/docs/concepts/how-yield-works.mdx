---
title: How Yield Works
description: Where the yield comes from and how it reaches your users.
---

EarnIT deposits flow into **USYC**, Circle's onchain representation of a short-duration US-treasury fund. USYC earns roughly the overnight rate through reverse-repo agreements backed by US government securities.

## The path of a deposit

1. A user deposits a stablecoin through your app.
2. EarnIT routes it into the **ERC-4626 USYC strategy** on Arc.
3. The strategy subscribes to USYC via Circle's teller.
4. A keeper calls `report()` on a schedule, realizing the accrued rate as profit.
5. Profit streams into the vault **share price** over an unlock window, so each share is worth progressively more of the underlying asset.

Your users never claim or compound — their position value rises as the share price rises.

## Share price, not rebasing

Positions are denominated in **vault shares**. Value is `shares × sharePrice`. As yield accrues, `sharePrice` increases; share balances stay constant. This keeps accounting clean and avoids rebasing surprises.

<Callout type="info">
  The profit-unlock window also blocks flash-loan APY manipulation — see [Security](/security).
</Callout>
