---
title: Fees & Revenue Share
description: How EarnIT charges and how partners earn.
---

EarnIT charges a single, transparent fee and shares revenue with partners.

- **Performance fee** — a flat **5%** of realized yield. No seat fees, no minimums, no per-call charges.
- **Partner revenue share** — you keep a configurable share of the yield your users generate. It accrues continuously and is paid out on a schedule.

## Worked example

| Item | Value |
| --- | --- |
| User deposits | $100,000 |
| Gross APY | ~9% |
| Annual gross yield | ~$9,000 |
| EarnIT performance fee (5%) | ~$450 |
| Net to ecosystem | ~$8,550 |

Your fee share is taken from the performance fee, not from your users' yield. See live numbers in the [dashboard](/dashboard) and model your own book on the [pricing page](/pricing).
