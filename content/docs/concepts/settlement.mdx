---
title: Settlement on Arc
description: Why EarnIT runs on Arc and what that means for integrators.
---

EarnIT settles on **Arc**, Circle's stablecoin-native L2.

- **USDC is the native gas token** — fees are predictable in dollar terms, and there is no volatile gas asset to manage.
- **Sub-second blocks, deterministic finality** — deposits and redemptions confirm fast with BFT consensus.
- **24/7 / T+0 redemptions** — USYC subscriptions and redemptions settle atomically in real time.

## What integrators need

| Parameter | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Chain ID | `5042002` (testnet) |
| RPC | `https://rpc.testnet.arc.network` |
| Explorer | `https://testnet.arcscan.app` |
| Native / transfer asset | USDC |

<Callout type="info">
  On Arc, the native USDC interface uses 18 decimals while the ERC-20 interface uses 6. Always read `decimals()` and never mix the two raw values.
</Callout>
